The second major factor which shapes my outlook is culture. Culture, for me, is anything from contributing to Wikipedia, particpating in local meetups, to creating art.

Arduino and organizations like Adafruit Industries have provided a stunning array of tools for the tinkerer in us all that help blur the line between technology and fashion, while my technical education has given me the skills to give back to my community in a meaninful way. I'm currently collaborating on a series of wearable electronic pieces, with more to come as we move from design to prototyping. I also volunteer regularly with the at a local NYC Parks Computer Resource Center, providing software training for patrons. This volunteering can range from general machine servicing to software seminars.

Last, but not least, culture plays a hige role in the work I hope to accomplish. Information organized engagingly that carries meaningful and interesting content is what I live for.
